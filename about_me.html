About Me

Who Am I?

I am Kheyl Pelayo from Singapore Polytechnic. My hobbies are playing basketball, graphic deigning and playing the guitar.

My Passion

My passion for eco-friendly living is I want to live a zero-waste lifestyle as I want to reduce the amount of waste that is being produced by me to help contribute towards the environment. This also helps me to be able to encourage others to take up the same habit

My Experience with Waste Reduction

I have multiple experiences with waste reduction. I had experiences with using recyclable cloth bags instead of plastic bags when going grocery shopping. Other experiences I had with reducing waste are with fast food restaurants like McDonald's using paper straws and wooden spoons instead of plastic ones so that they can be recycled instead of being wasted

My Favourite Green Hacks that I have Tried

My favourite “green hacks” that I have tried are turning my old shirt into a tote bag and collecting non-drinking water to fill up the washing machine so that less water is used to fill up the washing machine and the non-water is not wasted into the drain.
